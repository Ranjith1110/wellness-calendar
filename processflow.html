Benefits to Corporates

  • Actionable Data: Corporate Health Scorecard offers precise analytics by department and risk level.
  • Targeted Checkups: Receive AI-recommended annual health packages aligned with employee risk segmentation.
  • Cost Efficiency: Early detection reduces future claims and chronic management costs.
  • Productivity Boost: Fewer absentee days and improved morale due to better workforce health.
  • Employer Branding: Recognition as a Health-Conscious Employer enhances brand reputation and retention.
  • Broker Support: Integrated review and communication with Vizza simplifies implementation and reporting.
  • Preventive Strategy: End-to-end support from data to engagement for measurable wellness outcomes.

Benefits to Employees

  • Self-Awareness: Receive personal HRA report with actionable recommendations.
  • Access to Health Checks: Get corporate-sponsored preventive checkups based on personal risk level.
  • Guided Wellness: Participate in targeted webinars, consultations, and fitness challenges.
  • Continuous Support: Access digital tools and reminders to stay on top of health goals.
  • Empowerment: Feel valued and cared for through corporate investment in wellbeing.
